About

A shop, a laugh, and a love letter to Kiwi bogan culture.

The mission

bogan.co.nz exists to celebrate a culture that gets misread. We make wearables and gifts that feel accurate — not mean, not snobby, not generic “funny tee” junk.

What you can expect

  • Limited runs and signature drops.
  • Design that’s clean enough to wear in public.
  • Humour that doesn’t punch down.
  • A growing Field Guide full of local lore.
Collabs welcome: bands, garages, tattooists, local legends, small towns with big opinions.
