Soft bogans in the wild

They look corporate. They sound like Shihad in the carpark.

Field Guide • 4 min read

The soft bogan is a beautiful creature: a person who can do spreadsheets, wear clean shoes, and still get misty-eyed at a well-timed guitar solo.

Key indicators

  • Owns at least one “nice” jacket and one “shed” jacket.
  • Knows the words to songs they pretend they don’t like.
  • Says “not bad” while meaning “excellent.”
  • Has strong opinions about speakers and/or tyres.
Respect protocol: Don’t call them soft bogan to their face. They will deny it. You will both know the truth.
